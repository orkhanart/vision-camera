## The Post-Photography Revolution Has a Problem

Artists around the world are creating stunning AI art with tools like MidJourney, Runway, and Stable Diffusion. But there's a catch: the workflow is broken.

**The Current Reality:**
1. Take a photo with your phone or camera
2. Transfer it to your computer
3. Upload to an AI platform
4. Wait for processing
5. Download and iterate
6. Repeat until satisfied

**This kills creativity.** The spontaneous moment is lost. The inspiration fades. The friction between capture and creation breaks the artistic flow.

Mobile apps try to help, but they're limited by phone hardware and can't run full diffusion models. You get simplified results, not the quality you need.

**Desktop tools are powerful but stationary.** You can't bring MidJourney to the street, to the mountaintop, or to that perfect moment.
