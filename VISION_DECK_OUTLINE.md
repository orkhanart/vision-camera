## 2. THE PROBLEM
- Post-photography is here: AI art tools (MidJourney, Runway, Stable Diffusion) have revolutionized creative expression
- Current workflow is broken and time-consuming:
  1. Capture photo with phone/camera
  2. Transfer to computer
  3. Upload to AI platform
  4. Process and iterate
  5. Download and share
- Artists lose creative momentum during this multi-step process
- Desktop-only tools limit spontaneity and on-location creativity
- Mobile apps lack the computational power for high-quality diffusion models
- The gap: Real-time post-photography doesn't exist... until now

---

## 3. THE SOLUTION
- VISION: The world's first real-time post-photography camera
- Breakthrough: Full diffusion models running on edge device
- Capture and transform instantly - no computer, no waiting, no workflow friction
- Post-photography art in your hands, in real-time
- From concept to final art in seconds, not hours

## 8. COMPETITIVE LANDSCAPE

**Desktop AI Tools (MidJourney, Runway, Stable Diffusion)**
- Pros: Powerful, high-quality output
- Cons: Requires computer, multi-step workflow, not portable, kills creative momentum

**Mobile AI Apps**
- Pros: Portable, convenient
- Cons: Limited processing power, simplified models, lower quality, still requires multi-step process

**Traditional Cameras + Post-Processing**
- Pros: High-quality capture
- Cons: Separate workflow, time-consuming, requires technical expertise

**VISION Advantages:**
- Only device with full diffusion models on edge hardware
- Real-time post-photography - instant results
- Portable and spontaneous creativity
- Purpose-built for AI art workflow
- No computer or cloud required
- Professional quality in a handheld device
